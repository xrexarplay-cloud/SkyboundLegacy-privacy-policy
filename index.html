Skybound Legacy – Privacy Policy

Developed by XR Horizon Pvt. Ltd.
Effective Date: 10 October 2025

1. Introduction

XR Horizon Pvt. Ltd. (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Skybound Legacy, a single-player VR bowling game for Meta Quest devices, processes user data.

2. Data Collection Overview

Skybound Legacy does not collect, transmit, store on servers, share, or sell any personal data. No information is sent outside the user’s Meta Quest device.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details
  • Account credentials or login data
  • Location data
  • Biometric, health, or financial information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics, tracking, or telemetry data

4. Data Processed Locally on Device

The app processes and stores limited non-personal data locally on your device only:

  • Gameplay progress (scores, achievements, levels)
  • Player preferences (controls, audio, comfort settings)

This data is used solely to ensure normal game functionality and is never transmitted to XR Horizon or any third party.

5. Data Storage & Security

All game data remains on the user’s Meta Quest device. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no server-side data storage and no risk of external data breaches.

6. Data Deletion & User Rights

XR Horizon does not retain user data. Users may delete all locally stored game data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling Skybound Legacy from their Meta Quest device

For questions or data-related concerns, users may contact us using the details below.

7. Children’s Privacy

Skybound Legacy is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Third-Party Services

Skybound Legacy does not use third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, cloud storage, or external servers.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect platform or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
